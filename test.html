About Us

OZ Cycling Tours specializes in custom tours for riders of all ages and skill levels across the hundreds of miles of world-class Northwest Arkansas trails. We pride ourselves especially on our extensive knowledge of local gravel routes, and strive to make each rider feel supported, safe, and wowed with all our region has to offer to those on two wheels.

Co founders Adam and Ryan West, who grew up here in Northwest Arkansas had the privileged to watch OZ Trails grow and dig trail heads just down the street from their childhood home. Both have always lived and been fortunate to call this special place known as the “mountain bike capital of the world” home. “We Love NWA, the terrain, the sense of community, the towns, the people here, and we would love to share our a passion for cycling, these awesome trails, and community with you!”

We have a team of enthusiastic women and men ready to lead an awesome experience in the Ozarks!
